Mission Statement

Making the web accessible for everyone by providing developers with comprehensive accessibility testing tools directly in their browser.

üö® The Accessibility Challenge

üö´ Accessibility Barriers

Over 1 billion people worldwide have disabilities, yet most websites fail basic accessibility standards. This excludes a significant portion of the global population from digital experiences.

‚ö†Ô∏è Testing Challenges

Developers and testers lack integrated, real-time accessibility testing tools that can be seamlessly used during the development lifecycle.

üìã Compliance Issues

Organizations face significant hurdles in consistently meeting WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) and ADA (Americans with Disabilities Act) compliance requirements, leading to potential legal and reputational risks.

üîç Detection Gaps

Many existing accessibility tools often miss complex, context-specific issues and offer limited actionable guidance for remediation, leaving developers in the dark.

Solution: APEX - Your Accessibility Companion

APEX is a powerful Chrome Extension designed to empower developers, testers, and accessibility experts by providing comprehensive tools to scan web pages for accessibility compliance.

Comprehensive Scans

Quickly scan web pages for accessibility issues against established standards.

AI-Powered Suggestions (Gemini)

Leverages AI (powered by Gemini) to provide intelligent, context-aware suggestions for remediation.

Detailed PDF Reports

Generate professional PDF reports for documentation and sharing accessibility audit results.

üîñ Bookmarlets & Simulators

Includes various bookmarklets for quick checks and simulators to experience web content from different perspectives.
